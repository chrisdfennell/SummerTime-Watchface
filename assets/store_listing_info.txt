Summertime — Golden-Hour Beach Watch Face

(Alternates: "Summertime — Beach Digital Watch Face" /
 "Summertime: Tropical Beach Watch Face")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Summertime is a premium, high-fidelity digital watch face featuring a warm, relaxing golden-hour beach aesthetic. Experience a living sky, drifting clouds, rolling waves, and a swaying palm silhouette, all procedurally rendered. Large, outlined digital time and clean complications keep you connected and styled for summer.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• LIVING SKY GRADIENT: A procedural backdrop that shifts colors dynamically by time of day (sunrise pastels, midday azure, sunset marigold, and a starry deep night).
• ARCING SUN & MOON: Day/night orbital progression of a glowing sun (with rotating rays and bloom) and crescent silver moon.
• PROCEDURAL ANIMATIONS: Drifting fluffy clouds, rolling wave layers with physics, and a swaying palm tree silhouette at the beach.
• ROTATING SECONDS: A sliced orange second indicator floating and orbiting the perimeter.
• DYNAMIC COMPLICATIONS:
  - Heart Icon (coral-colored, 16px with black outline) + Body Battery percentage.
  - Water Droplet Icon (turquoise-colored, 12px with black outline) + device battery percentage.
  - Steps Bar (XP tracker, sand/gold shoreline) + numeric steps count.
• HIGH-CONTRAST OUTLINES: All texts (clock, date, weather, and battery metrics) feature a crisp black outline for absolute readability against the dynamic visual backdrops.

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
Summertime adapts to your panel:
• AMOLED (Fenix 8 / tactix 8 / quatix 8 and Fenix 8 Pro): smooth gradient resource fills, specular highlights, and active background illumination.
• MIP / Solar (tactix 8 Solar, Fenix 8 Solar): high-contrast flat fills and optimized backdrops tuned for maximum sunlight readability.
Everything is laid out relative to your screen, so it looks right on every supported case size.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim grey time/date, thin outline representations of the battery metrics, steps progress outline, and no visual fills or background animations. All lit pixels are shifted a few pixels each minute. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line.
• Step Goal Override — steps for a full shoreline bar; 0 uses the watch's own step goal.

PRIVACY
Summertime uses only the SensorHistory permission to read Body Battery. It does not collect or transmit any data off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
tactix 8 (AMOLED & Solar), Fenix 8 (43/47/51mm AMOLED), Fenix 8 Solar, Fenix 8 Pro, quatix 8, Forerunner 965.

Equip Summertime, and take a moment at the beach.
